But darling, I swear, it's not my rule.

But still, my love for you burns so bright.

Please be my Valentine, just for this day,
But know, my love, it won't go away.
I promise to never leave your side,
To hold you close, be your loving guide.

So be my Valentine, now and always,
Till the end of time, with you I'll stay.
You're the one for me, my love, my life,
